About Stock Wizard

For small investors or people interested in getting into investing, Stock Wizard makes it easier to get into the stock market by lowering the learning curve through innovative analysis tools and providing an accessible way to manage their portfolios. The value of our product lies in its ability to create reliable and profitable investment opportunities for our customers and help them find their way in investment as it can contain a steep learning curve at the beginning. Code is available on GitHub.

Background

We are 4 students from Istanbul Technical Universtity, taking the class BLG439E, to create a stock portfolio management tool for beginner investors. Code is available at

Our Vision

Our vision is to make it simple, especially for those who are just starting. We believe everyone should have the right to manage their investment by themselves in a democratic way.

Our Core Value

A platform with simple & intuitive user interfaces, complementary advice system. We want to make investing simple for everyone.
